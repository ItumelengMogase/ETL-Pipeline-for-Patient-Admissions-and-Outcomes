## Introduction

This project is an end-to-end ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) pipeline designed to process artificial patient admission and medical data. The pipeline uses **Apache Airflow** for orchestration, **PostgreSQL** for data storage, **dbt** for data transformation, and **Power BI** for data visualisation. 

The dataset is synthetic and designed to mimic real-world medical records, encompassing demographic, diagnostic, and procedural data. This project automates the ingestion of raw data, its transformation into structured formats, and storage in PostgreSQL for querying and visualisation.

---

## The Business Case

Healthcare facilities and research institutions often need insights derived from patient data to improve care quality, optimise resource allocation, and conduct impactful research. However, access to real-world patient data is often restricted due to privacy regulations. This project addresses that gap by offering a scalable and customisable synthetic dataset alongside an ETL pipeline, enabling the extraction of actionable insights.

### Use Cases:
- Monitoring trends in patient demographics and health outcomes.
- Analysing the effectiveness of treatments or medical procedures.
- Studying regional disparities in healthcare access and quality.

---

## The Problem

Handling real-world medical data presents challenges:
1. **Data Privacy**: Strict privacy laws such as HIPAA or POPIA limit access to patient data.
2. **Data Complexity**: Medical records involve intricate relationships between demographics, diagnoses, treatments, and outcomes.
3. **Data Processing**: Scaling ETL processes to handle millions of records requires efficient data management and transformation tools.

---

## The Solution

This project tackles these challenges with:
- **Synthetic Patient Data**: Mimics real-world medical records without privacy concerns.
- **Modern Tools**:
  - **Apache Airflow**: Orchestrates data ingestion workflows.
  - **PostgreSQL**: Serves as a reliable, scalable database for raw and transformed data.
  - **dbt**: Automates data cleaning and transformation.
  - **Power BI**: Visualises insights into patient admissions and health outcomes.
